Workshop Information

Below, you will be able to sign up for our Fall 2022 workshops. For each course, you will find information regarding whether it will be held virtually, in-person at the Rockville Memorial Library, or using a hybrid fashion (those who wish to come in person can; otherwise people can join on Zoom). Each workshop will be held on a different day of the week, so please don't hesitate to sign up for multiple!

The best part? Everything's free!
